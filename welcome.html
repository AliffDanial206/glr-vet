We set up our veterinary clinic in Shah Alam, and have opened the doors of our practice to pets in the community ever since. Our patients include dogs, cats, rabbits, hamsters and other small pets. Along with veterinary treatment for your pet, we focus on preventive medicine for animals of all ages and are deeply committed to the health and well-being of your pets.

Our team of veterinarians and support staff are committed to providing quality and compassionate care for your pets.
